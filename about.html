About Martly

We’re on a mission to make style accessible, joyful, and effortless — for everyone.

Our Story

Martly was born in 2022 from a simple idea: shopping should feel like discovery, not a chore. What started as a small pop‑up in Brooklyn has grown into a global platform connecting independent designers, curated brands, and conscious consumers. We believe that great style doesn’t have to cost the earth — or your peace of mind.

What We Stand For

  • Curated with care: Every piece is chosen for quality, durability, and timeless appeal.
  • Inclusive & diverse: We celebrate all bodies, identities, and expressions.
  • Transparent & fair: We work with ethical suppliers and share our impact openly.

Our commitment: By 2027, we aim to make 80% of our products from sustainable or recycled materials. We’re also carbon‑neutral on all deliveries.

Why Martly?

  • Style meets substance: Fashion that looks good and feels good.
  • Community first: We listen to our customers and co‑create collections with them.
  • Hassle‑free: Easy returns, fast shipping, and a support team that actually cares.
