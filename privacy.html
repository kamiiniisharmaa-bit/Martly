Privacy Policy

At Martly, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, shipping/billing address.
  • Payment data: Credit/debit card details, digital wallet info (processed securely via PCI-compliant partners).
  • Usage data: Browsing behaviour, purchase history, IP address, device type, and cookies.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process orders, payments, and deliver products.
  • To personalise your shopping experience and recommend products.
  • To send order updates, promotional offers (you can opt out anytime).
  • To improve our website, app, and customer support.

Your rights: You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us at privacy@martly.com.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for site functionality, and optional analytics/advertising cookies with your consent. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We share data with trusted service providers (payment gateways, shipping carriers, analytics) solely to fulfil orders and improve our services.
  • We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of Martly, our users, or others.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS), access controls, and regular security audits to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information.

6. International Transfers

Martly operates globally. Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States, where data protection laws may differ. We ensure appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of Martly after changes constitutes acceptance.
